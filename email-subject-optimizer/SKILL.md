# Email Subject Optimizer

## Description

An AI-powered email subject line optimizer that generates high-converting subject lines, creates A/B test variations, and improves open rates through psychology-based copywriting techniques. Craft subject lines that get opened.

## When to Use

- Crafting email campaign subject lines
- Writing newsletter titles
- Creating cold outreach emails
- A/B testing email variations
- Improving open rates
- Writing promotional emails

## Psychology Principles

### 1. Curiosity Gap
Create mystery that compels opening:
- "The one thing most people miss..."
- "You won't believe this..."
- "What nobody tells you about..."

### 2. Personalization
Use recipient's context:
- "[Name], your report is ready"
- "Your weekly insights"
- "John, quick question about..."

### 3. Urgency & Scarcity
Drive immediate action:
- "Last chance: ends tonight"
- "Only 3 spots left"
- "Limited time offer"

### 4. Value Proposition
Clear benefit statement:
- "How to [achieve result] in [timeframe]"
- "Save X hours with this..."
- "Free [valuable thing] inside"

### 5. Social Proof
Leverage authority:
- "Industry leaders are using..."
- "Trusted by 10,000+ users"
- "As seen in [publication]"

## Best Practices

### DO
- Keep under 50 characters (mobile)
- Front-load important words
- Use preview text effectively
- Test emoji (sparingly)
- Create urgency when genuine
- Personalize when possible

### DON'T
- Use ALL CAPS
- Excessive punctuation!!!
- Clickbait (damages trust)
- Misleading claims
- Generic greetings
- Spam trigger words

## A/B Testing Framework

Generate variations across dimensions:
1. Length (short vs. descriptive)
2. Style (formal vs. casual)
3. Format (question vs. statement)
4. Personalization level
5. Urgency level
