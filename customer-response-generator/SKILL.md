# Customer Response Generator

## What It Does
Generate professional, empathetic customer service responses for any situation. Handle complaints, inquiries, shipping questions, refunds, and general support with confidence.

## How to Use
1. Describe the customer inquiry or complaint
2. Include relevant details (order numbers, products, timeline)
3. Specify your company's policies if relevant
4. Choose tone level (formal/friendly/urgent)
5. Get a polished, professional response

## Response Types

### Complaints & Issues
- Product defects/damage
- Late deliveries
- Wrong items received
- Service dissatisfaction
- Billing disputes

### Information Requests
- Order status inquiries
- Product details
- Return policies
- Shipping information
- Account issues

### Positive Feedback
- Thank you responses
- Review acknowledgments
- Loyalty rewards
- Referral requests

## Example

**Customer Complaint**: "My order arrived damaged and it's been 2 weeks with no response"

**Generated Response**:
```
Subject: Re: Your Recent Order - We're Sorry

Dear [Customer Name],

I'm truly sorry to hear about your experience with your recent order. This is not the standard of service we strive to provide, and I understand your frustration.

I've reviewed your order #[12345] and I'm expediting a replacement shipment today via express delivery. You'll receive tracking information within 24 hours.

Additionally, I'd like to offer you a [X]% discount on your next order as our sincere apology.

Please don't hesitate to reach out if there's anything else I can help with.

Warm regards,
[Your Name]
Customer Support Team
```

## Key Principles
- Acknowledge the issue first
- Show genuine empathy
- Take ownership proactively
- Provide clear solutions
- Set realistic expectations
- Include next steps and timeline
