# Resume Optimizer

## What It Does
Transform your resume into a powerful job-search tool. Get ATS-friendly formatting, compelling bullet points, and keyword optimization that helps your resume pass screening systems and catch recruiters' attention.

## How to Use
1. Share your current resume (or key information)
2. Describe your target job/industry
3. Highlight your key achievements
4. Mention specific companies or roles
5. Get an optimized, professional resume

## What You Get

### Structure Optimization
- Clean, professional layout
- ATS-compatible formatting
- Strategic section ordering
- Consistent formatting throughout

### Content Enhancement
- Powerful action verbs
- Quantified achievements
- Industry-specific keywords
- Relevant skills highlighting
- Customized summary/objective

### ATS Optimization
- Keyword matching with job descriptions
- Clean formatting (no tables/graphics)
- Standard section headers
- Proper file format recommendations

## Example

**Before**:
```
• Helped customers with their problems
• Worked on team projects
• Used Microsoft Office
```

**After**:
```
• Resolved 50+ daily customer inquiries, achieving 95% satisfaction rating
• Collaborated with 5-person cross-functional team to launch new product line
• Created automated Excel dashboards reducing reporting time by 3 hours weekly
```

## Resume Sections

### Essential
- Contact information
- Professional summary (3-4 sentences)
- Work experience
- Education

### Recommended
- Skills (technical & soft)
- Certifications
- Projects

### Optional
- Languages
- Volunteer work
- Interests (selective)

## Tips
- Tailor resume to each application
- Use numbers to quantify impact
- Lead with results, not duties
- Keep it to 1-2 pages
- Use PDF unless specified otherwise
